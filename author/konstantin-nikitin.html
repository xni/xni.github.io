Koding.com is awesome!

Published: вс 19 июля 2015
By Konstantin Nikitin

In Review.

I have just tried koding.com and it looks like a miracle. It gives me an ability to setup some environment, e.g. packages, libraries, scripts, and then to share it to my collegues, who can start making changes or experiments immediately.

Yes, I know, that that kind of functionality may be achieved by using docker, docker registry and so on, but it is not so collaborative and cool.
